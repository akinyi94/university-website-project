Our Campus

Jiinue University was founded in 1945 on a 61 acre land that was donated to us by the Government of Kenya. The Government of Kenya mandated us to take over the college. In 1946, the faculty of Mechanical Enginnering established Bachelors of Mechanical Engineering as its first degree programme within the camopus. On 6th January 2001,former president Moi through legal notice No 226 granted Jiinue University Charter in accordance to the Universities Act 1999
